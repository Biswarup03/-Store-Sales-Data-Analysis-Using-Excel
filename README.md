# 📊 Store Sales Data Analysis Using Excel

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a retail store dataset using **Microsoft Excel** to uncover business insights, customer behavior patterns, and sales performance trends. The analysis was performed using Excel features such as Pivot Tables, Charts, Data Cleaning, and Dashboard Visualization.

The dataset contains over **31,000 sales records** with details related to customer demographics, order status, product categories, sales channels, and regional sales distribution.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze monthly sales and order trends
* Compare purchasing behavior between men and women
* Study order status distribution
* Identify top-performing states/cities
* Analyze customer age groups and gender contribution
* Evaluate sales channels performance
* Build an interactive Excel dashboard for business insights

## Key Insights

### 📈 Sales Analysis

* Monthly sales and order trends were analyzed to identify peak business periods.
* Seasonal purchasing patterns were observed across different months.

### 👥 Customer Insights

* Female customers contributed a larger share of total sales compared to male customers.
* Adult customers formed the majority of purchases.

### 🚚 Order Status Analysis

* Most orders were successfully delivered.
* A smaller percentage of orders were cancelled, returned, or refunded.

### 🌍 Regional Performance

* Maharashtra and Karnataka were among the top-performing states in terms of revenue generation.
* Sales distribution across multiple cities and states was analyzed.

### 🛒 Sales Channel Analysis

* Amazon and Myntra contributed significantly to total orders.
* Multiple online channels were compared for performance evaluation.
